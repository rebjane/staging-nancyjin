Taxation Services for Business

Corporate Tax Planning

Our advisor team has sophisticated experience in corporate tax planning and is skillful on the tax related issues generated from shares’ structure designing, corporate restructuring, mergers and acquisitions etc.

Prepare corporate income tax returns

Our tax accountants will check the year end financial statements and adjust the net income to taxable income. We will also identify the active business income and passive income to apply different tax rates. After we filed the tax returns to CRA, we will reminder you to pay the tax owing and instalment payments.

The key issues to save your money and time when CRA required to audit your corporate tax returns are your organized book-keeping records. Please be careful in keeping all your original documents at least 5 years.

Scientific Research & Experimental Development (SR&ED) Tax Credits and Government Grants

In Canada, the government encourages technological innovations and offers various incentives, if your company invested in IT, manufacturing, green agriculture or bio-pharmaceuticals etc., you might be entitled to SR&ED tax credits.

Our tax team will help to assess your eligibilities and help you to claim entitled tax credits and to apply qualified government grants.

GST/HST Consulting

Our tax team will learn to know your business activities and processes and use our expertise to assist you on GST/HST collections and GST/HST input tax credits (ITCs) claims.

Every year, CRA puts a lot of efforts on GST/HST review , we are well-experienced on handling these issues and can help business owners to manage the risks.
